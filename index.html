About Me

I'm a passionate teacher in the Computer Science and Engineering department at Jashore University of Science and Technology (JUST) in Bangladesh. With over two years of experience in this field, I specialize in programming and research, particularly in reinforcement learning for transportation systems. Driven by a love for teaching and continuous learning, I strive to motivate my students in ways that make a real impact-not only in their personal lives but also in society as a whole. My goal is to inspire curiosity, foster critical thinking, and help students realize their full potential.
